# Phone Changelog

## [Initial Version] - {PR_MERGE_DATE}

- Search macOS Contacts and dial the highlighted contact with `↩`.
- Argument support: `Call <name>` at the Raycast root auto-dials when there is a single match.
- FaceTime Audio (`⌘A`), FaceTime Video (`⌘V`), and Copy Number (`⌘C`) actions.
- Frecency ranking of recent calls.
- 24-hour contact cache with manual refresh (`⌘R` or the **Refresh Contacts** command).
